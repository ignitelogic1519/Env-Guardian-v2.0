Privacy Policy

What the Env Guardian app collects, why it collects it, who can see it — and, just as important, what it never touches.

Effective date: 15 July 2026

Who we are & what this app is

Env Guardian is a workplace-security application. Your employer (or the organisation whose premises you access — "the organisation") asks you to install it on your Android device so that, while your device is physically inside a defined restricted zone, only approved apps can be used. Outside the zone the app stands down and your phone behaves normally.

This policy covers the Env Guardian mobile app and the data it sends to the organisation's dashboard. The app is provided by Env Guardian ("we"). For any privacy question, contact ignite.logic1519@gmail.com.

Data we collect and why

  • ✔ Identity you enter at setup — your name and employee ID. Used to register the device to you on the organisation's dashboard.
  • ✔ Device information — device model, Android version and a device identifier. Used to identify the device on the dashboard and support troubleshooting.
  • ✔ Location, including in the background — GPS position, checked continuously so the app knows whether the device is inside or outside the restricted zone. Location is the on/off switch for all enforcement.
  • ✔ Installed apps list — the packages installed on the device, so the organisation's allow-list can be applied.
  • ✔ App usage time inside the zone — how long approved apps are used while inside the restricted zone, when the organisation has enabled per-app time limits.
  • ✔ Enforcement events — records of blocked app launches, tamper attempts (e.g. trying to disable or uninstall the app), and QR presence verification inside the zone.
  • ✔ Device status telemetry — battery level/charging state, permission compliance status, and a periodic heartbeat confirming the app is running.

What the app never collects

The app cannot and does not: read your messages, e-mails, photos, files, contacts or call logs; record audio or video; read or intercept the content of your network traffic (its VPN feature only decides which apps may reach the internet inside the zone — it does not inspect, store or route traffic content); or track which websites you visit. The camera is used only when you actively scan the zone-entry QR code, and notification access is used only to detect which apps are running or in a call — never to read notification contents beyond that purpose.

Outside the restricted zone, enforcement is inactive. App blocking, the network guard and usage counting operate only inside the zone. Location continues to be checked outside the zone solely to detect when you enter it, and your inside/outside status is visible to the organisation.

Special Android permissions, explained

  • ✔ Accessibility service — detects app launches inside the zone so non-approved apps can be closed. Not used to read personal content.
  • ✔ VPN — a local on-device filter that cuts network access for non-approved apps inside the zone. No traffic content is read or logged.
  • ✔ Usage access — measures in-zone screen time per app for the organisation's time limits.
  • ✔ Notification access — identifies which apps are active in the background before zone check-in.
  • ✔ Display over other apps — shows the blocking screen when a non-approved app is opened in the zone.
  • ✔ Camera — scans the zone-entrance QR code to verify physical presence.

Who sees your data, sharing & retention

Your organisation's administrators see the data described above on their dashboard: your registration details, device status, inside/outside-zone state, enforcement logs and in-zone usage. That is the product's purpose and the only audience.

We do not sell your data, use it for advertising, or share it with third parties. Data is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS) to the organisation's server and stored there. Enforcement logs are kept for a limited operational window (by default around 10 days) before being purged; registration and status data are kept while your device remains enrolled.

Deletion: uninstalling the app stops all collection immediately. To have stored dashboard data about you removed, ask your organisation's administrator, or contact us at the e-mail below and we will coordinate the deletion.

Your choices & consent

Every permission is requested explicitly during setup, with a disclosure shown in-app before background location is enabled. Enrollment is something your organisation asks of you as a condition of accessing its restricted premises; if you do not consent, do not complete setup, and speak to your organisation about alternatives. The app is intended for working adults (18+), not children.

Changes & contact

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this address with a new effective date. Material changes will also be reflected in the app's store listing.

Questions, concerns or deletion requests: ignite.logic1519@gmail.com
