FAQ

Common questions

Do employees have to factory-reset or enroll their phones?

No. Env Guardian runs as a normal app with granted permissions — no device wipe, no corporate enrollment. That's the whole point of the BYOD-friendly design.

Is personal data or activity outside the zone tracked?

No. Enforcement only activates inside the geofenced zone. Outside it, the phone behaves completely normally and nothing personal is collected.

What happens if someone disables the app or VPN?

It's detected. Disabling the enforcer, accessibility service or VPN raises a tamper signal that's reported to your dashboard and can trigger an auto-lock.

Which devices are supported?

Android across major OEMs. We include guided auto-start & battery setup for aggressive skins (Xiaomi, Oppo, Realme, Vivo, Samsung) to keep the monitor alive.

Can we manage it without a big dashboard?

Yes. Zones, whitelists and time limits can be configured directly in the database today, with a web dashboard on the roadmap.

How much does it cost to run?

The backend and database run comfortably on free tiers (Render + Neon). The app is distributed privately as an APK.
