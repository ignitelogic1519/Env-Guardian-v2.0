The BYOD blind spot

You can't wipe a phone you don't own.

Employees carry personal phones into labs, exam halls, data centres and secure floors — with cameras, cloud uploads and endless distractions. Traditional MDM means enrolling or factory-resetting the device, which nobody agrees to for a personal phone. So the risk goes unmanaged. Env Guardian closes that gap by enforcing policy based on location — without ever owning the device.

How it works

Four steps, fully automatic

No admin babysitting. The device enforces itself the moment it crosses your geofence.

1

Enroll once

The employee installs the app, grants permissions, and the device is sealed and permanently bound to their identity (anti-theft: no one else can re-register it).

2

Enter the zone

A geofence trigger brings the app forward. The employee scans a physical QR — static or time-rotating — to prove they're actually there.

3

Enforce automatically

Only whitelisted apps run. Per-app daily time limits apply. Unapproved apps lose their internet. Every allow/block is logged.

4

Leave & restore

Cross the boundary and full access returns instantly — while compliance and activity are reported back to your server in real time.

Capabilities

Everything you need to hold a perimeter

📍

Geofenced zones

Define any restricted area as a polygon. Enforcement activates only inside it.

🛡

Zero-trust app blocking

Non-whitelisted apps are pushed off-screen instantly by an on-device enforcer.

⏱

Per-app time limits

Allow an app, but only for X minutes a day — measured and enforced on-device.

🔐

QR presence auth

Verify physical presence with a static or rotating (TOTP) zone QR code.

🌐

Network guard

Cut internet to unapproved apps while in-zone with a local, no-root VPN.

🚫

Anti-theft binding

A device is locked to its first owner — a stolen phone can't be re-claimed.

📊

Live compliance

Real-time telemetry, compliance scoring, and remote lock — all server-driven.

⚙️

No device wipe

Runs on personal phones with granted permissions — no reset, no enrollment friction.
