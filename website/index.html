The BYOD blind spot

You can't wipe a phone you don't own.

Employees carry personal phones into labs, exam halls, data centres and secure floors — with cameras, cloud uploads and endless distractions. Traditional MDM means enrolling or factory-resetting the device, which nobody accepts for a personal phone. So the risk goes unmanaged. Env Guardian enforces policy by location — without ever owning the device.

Capabilities

Everything you need to hold a perimeter

Eight enforcement layers, all automatic inside your zone.

📍

Geofenced zones

Define any restricted area as a polygon. Enforcement activates only inside it.

🛡️

Zero-trust blocking

Non-whitelisted apps are pushed off-screen instantly by an on-device enforcer.

⏱️

Per-app time limits

Allow an app, but only for X minutes a day — measured and enforced on-device.

🔐

QR presence auth

Verify physical presence with a static or rotating (TOTP) zone QR code.

🌐

Network guard

Cut internet to unapproved apps in-zone with a local, no-root VPN.

🚫

Anti-theft binding

A device is locked to its first owner — a stolen phone can't be re-claimed.

📊

Live compliance

Real-time telemetry, compliance scoring and remote lock — server-driven.

⚙️

No device wipe

Runs on personal phones with granted permissions — no reset, no enrollment.

How it works

Four steps, fully automatic

1

Enroll once

Install, grant permissions, and the device is sealed & bound to the employee.

2

Enter the zone

A geofence trigger brings the app forward — scan the QR to prove presence.

3

Enforce

Only approved apps run; limits apply; unapproved apps lose network; all logged.

4

Leave & restore

Cross the boundary and full access returns instantly. Compliance is reported.
