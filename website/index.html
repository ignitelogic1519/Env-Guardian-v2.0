The BYOD blind spot

You can't wipe a phone you don't own.

Employees carry personal phones into labs, exam halls, data centres and secure floors — with cameras, cloud uploads and endless distractions. Traditional MDM means enrolling or factory-resetting the device, which nobody accepts for a personal phone. So the risk goes unmanaged.

  • ✕ Full MDM enrollment feels invasive on a personal device
  • ✕ A blanket "no phones" rule is impossible to police
  • ✓ Env Guardian enforces policy by location — never owning the device
The boundary is the switch

One step changes everything

Cross the geofence and the device flips state in seconds — automatically, both ways.

🟢 Outside the zone

A normal personal phone

  • ✓ Every app works, full internet
  • ✓ No monitoring of personal use
  • ✓ The VPN is off; nothing is blocked
🔴 Inside the zone

A locked-down secure device

  • ✓ Only whitelisted apps run
  • ✓ Unapproved apps lose internet (always-on VPN)
  • ✓ QR presence check + live compliance logging
Capabilities

Everything you need to hold a perimeter

Eight enforcement layers, enforced natively on-device — they keep working even with the app closed.

📍

Geofenced zones

Draw any restricted area as a polygon. Enforcement arms itself only inside it, disarms outside.

🛡️

Zero-trust blocking

Non-whitelisted apps are pushed off-screen instantly by an on-device accessibility enforcer.

⏱️

Per-app time limits

Allow an app, but only for X minutes a day — measured from real usage and enforced on-device.

🔐

QR presence auth

Confirm a person is physically present with a static or rotating (TOTP) zone QR code.

🌐

Always-on network guard

A local, no-root VPN cuts internet to unapproved apps in-zone and switches off the moment you leave.

🚫

Anti-theft binding

A device is bound to its first owner — a lost or stolen phone can't be re-registered by someone else.

📊

Live compliance

Real-time location, permission and tamper telemetry, a compliance score and one-tap remote lock.

⚙️

No device wipe

Runs on personal phones using only user-granted permissions — no reset, no full-device enrollment.

How it works

Four steps, fully automatic

Set up once. After that the device enforces itself and streams status back in real time.

1

Enroll once

Install, grant the required permissions, and the device is sealed & bound to the employee.

2

Enter the zone

A geofence trigger brings the app forward — scan the QR to prove you're really there.

3

Enforce

Only approved apps run; time limits apply; unapproved apps lose internet; everything is logged.

4

Leave & restore

Cross the boundary and full access returns within seconds. The VPN drops; compliance is reported.
